# ADR-002 — Enrollment identity-assurance tiers

**Status:** Accepted (2026-07-16).

## Context
Not every enrolled subject carries the same evidentiary weight. A self-asserted enrollment
and a union-vouched one should not look identical to an underwriter.

## Decision
Tiered assurance on enrollment. **Fiduciary-vouched** is the premium tier and the intended
fast-follow: once a workplace organisation becomes a fiduciary, its vouching turns it from an
endorser into an **entitlement root**, not merely a signatory.

## Consequences
- Gives insurers and studios a graded trust signal.
- Creates the concrete upgrade path that makes a union want to become a fiduciary.
